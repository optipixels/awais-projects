A LITTLE BIT ABOUT US

The firm Fancy Engravers was established in 1967, by Late Muhammad Asif Mirza. His younger brotherMuhammad Arif Mirza and Muhammad Maqsood Mirza joined him in 1970. The company started supplyingthe souvenirs products to the Army all over Pakistan. The Company is supplying products to all thethree Armed Forces, Air Force and Navy. Slowly and Gradually the company became the main supplierin all over Pakistan through out Army. It achieved many milestones and its all about the Hard Work, Passion, Team Work and Enthusiasm at all the projects which leads to become the largest and main suppliers of Trophies, Shields, Plaques, Cups, Miniature Models, Medal, Coins, Replicas, German Silver Items, Quarter Guard Items, Metal Badges, Flags, Ceremonial Poles, Leather Gift Items, Clocks and somany other major products.
